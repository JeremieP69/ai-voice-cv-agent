# Truckfly by Michelin

Truckfly by Michelin is a European mobile platform for truck drivers.

The product combines:
- POIs for truck drivers
- community features
- B2B opportunities
- paid truck navigation

## Scope

- 44 countries
- 21 languages
- 2.3M+ downloads
- GPS subscription launched in 7 months
- more than 4,000 users and more than 1,900 paying subscribers

## User problem framing

Truck drivers need reliable truck-specific navigation and relevant stops on the road. Generic navigation tools do not always account for truck constraints or driver-specific needs.

## Product decision

Launch a paid truck GPS subscription inside Truckfly, using the existing driver community and POI platform as the distribution and product foundation.

## Business objective

Create a monetization layer while increasing the product’s value for drivers.

## Outcome

The GPS subscription was launched in 7 months and reached more than 4,000 users and more than 1,900 paying subscribers.

## Iteration

After launch, Jérémie and the team used user feedback, support issues, product analytics and field insights to identify recurring pain points, improve reliability and iterate.
